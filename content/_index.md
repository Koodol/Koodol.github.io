I am an M.S. student at the [DS Lab](https://sundong.kim/), Gwangju Institute of Science
and Technology (GIST), advised by Prof. Sundong Kim.

I'm currently working on using LLMs to solve abstract reasoning tasks such as the
[ARC-AGI](https://arcprize.org/arc-agi) benchmark, with a focus on reasoning
strategies that generalize rather than overfit to a single benchmark. Previously,
I worked on platform-invariant topic modeling and small language models for
dialogue and text classification (see [Publications](/publications/)).
